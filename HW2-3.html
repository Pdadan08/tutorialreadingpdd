Besides learning the IELTS exam preparation tips and strategies, being aware of the common mistakes that other test takers while answering IELTS Reading sentence completion make can be helpful. So, we have listed the four most common mistakes below.
  • Neglecting to recognize synonyms or paraphrased terms in headings and passages
  • Choosing headings that are too broad or too narrow, does not accurately reflect the paragraphs' main idea.
  • Selecting a heading that does not align with the overall theme or argument of the paragraph.
